How to make tie dye a tapestry

here is the step by step guide in creating your own tie-dyed tapestry!

step 1: purchase blank bedsheets, preferably cotton. gather other necessary supplies include rubber bands, empty bottles, gloves, dye.

step 2: soak your sheet in warm water for a couple of minutes

step 3: fill the bottles with mixed tie-dye

step 4: remove your sheet from the water and wring them out

step 5: twist, fold, or wrap the sheet with rubber bands

step 6: start dyeing. soak or apply color. when finished, put the sheet in a plastic ziploc bag

step 7: wait at least 12 hours for the dye to be absorbed completely

step 8: cut off the rubber bands

step 9: rinse the dyed sheet under water

step 10: wring the sheet gently and then hang to dry

step 11: wash the completed sheet in cold water, make sure to wash it alone to avoid bleeding

you are done!
